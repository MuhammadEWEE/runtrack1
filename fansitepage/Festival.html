    • Songkran : Place and date

      Songkran is a traditional festival which marks the Buddhist new year and whose dates refer to the change in position of the sun in the astrological sky. It takes place from April 13 to 15. A symbol of love, Songkran helps strengthen the bonds that unite families, communities and Thai society as a whole.

    • Loy Kratong

      Every year, during the full moon of the 12th month of the lunar calendar ( this year November 15, 2024 ) the magnificent Loy Krathong festival takes place, otherwise known as the “Festival of Lights”. For this occasion, Thailand shines brightly for three days of celebrations and ceremonies, each more beautiful than the last.
